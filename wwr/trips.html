Most Reserve Trips

The Roar River

The river's name, "Roar", is well-deserved as you'll soon discover. Its mighty waters surge with an unrelenting power, creating an exhilarating symphony of rushing rapids and cascading waterfalls. You'll be captivated by the resounding roar of the river as it rushes over ancient rocks and under moss-covered bridges.

The Furious Rapid

Furious Rapids is not for the faint of heart. This legendary stretch of river offers an exhilarating and challenging adventure for those seeking the ultimate thrill. Set in a breathtaking wilderness with rugged cliffs and dense forests as your backdrop, the very name "Furious Rapids" hints at the wild ride you're about to embark upon.

The Bullet River

Bullet River isn't just about speed; it's also a test of your courage, teamwork, and quick decision-making. You and your fellow rafters must paddle in perfect harmony, listening to your guide's commands as you navigate the tumultuous waves and powerful currents.

The Piranha Rapid

Piranha Rapids gets its name from the ferocious energy that courses through its tumultuous waters. This untamed river is a sight to behold. Towering emerald-green trees, vibrant tropical flowers, and the symphony of wildlife set the stage for an unforgettable experience.
